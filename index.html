About Me

Professional Teacher & Creative Designer

I am Gerry A. Ook, a passionate design specialist with over 10 years of experience in the creative industry. Currently, I serve as a dedicated teacher at JRIIT College, where I impart my knowledge and skills to the next generation of designers.

My expertise spans across various design disciplines, from brand identity creation to video editing. I believe in the power of visual communication to transform ideas into impactful experiences that resonate with audiences.

Whether you're looking to establish your brand with a professional logo, create eye-catching marketing materials, or develop engaging video content, I bring both technical expertise and creative vision to every project.
